About Us

UVPF Startup Labs is a brand name for the service wing of Udyam Vidya Prasar Foundation, which, in association with professionals working in skill development, entrepreneurship, innovation, and incubation, provides support and consultancy to establish an ecosystem for innovation and entrepreneurship in higher educational institutes (HEI) by making all the resources available. Udyam Vidya Prasar Foundation is a section 8 company with the objective of spreading the culture of innovation & entrepreneurship in society among youth by providing training, guidance, mentorship, and other possible support to promote research & development, arts, science, etc.

UVP Foundation

Udyam Vidya Prasar Foundation is a section 8 company, working in startup and entrepreneurship, skill development, education, research and development, innovation commercialization, and self-employment. It is an association of professionals who are working in skill development, innovation, incubation, and entrepreneurship for the last 17 years.
